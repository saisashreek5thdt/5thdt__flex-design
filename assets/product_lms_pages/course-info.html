Course Intro

Course Description

DreamShaper is an online Project Based Learning tool that guides students through practical and engaging learning experiences. When building projects related to their classes, students learn autonomously but with all the support and guidance of their teachers....

DreamShaper was created to help teachers implement or improve project-based teaching practices in their classes with a set of features and support materials that make everything very easy.

The tool allows teachers to define and monitor their students work and share support materials, written or video feedback, essential to help students in asynchronous project activities.

DreamShaper went through an impact study evaluation conducted by one of the most respected institutions in the world in Education, the Lemann Foundation, which proved the development of social emotional skills in students.

By promoting the introduction of active learning methodologies, the tool helps to ensure the development of skills as organization, leadership, planning, curiosity, critical thinking, creativity, among others.

What you will learn

What do I want to be and where do I want to go in the future?
What is the path I have to take to fulfill my goals?
Know their identity, skills, and natural strengths
Identify and mature their dreams and long term goals
Define an academic and professional plan to fulfill their goals

Curriculum

Section-1

Life Project Video preview 17 Min

Students develop a business idea related to a theme proposed by the teacher;
They build a business model and study the viability and sustainability of the idea;
They conduct interviews with the target audience of their idea;
They build a prototype that brings the project to life;
Develop Marketing, Operational and Financial plans.

Section-2

Entrepreneurship Project Video preview 50 Min

Students investigate a theme proposed by the teacher;
They define a starting problem related to this topic and their personal interests;
Deepen the knowledge about the problem through practical research methodologies;
They design, plan and test a viable solution to solve the starting problem.

Section-3

Research Based Project Video preview 32 Min

Students reflect on a topic related to the discipline and proposed by the teacher;
They propose a starting question related to this topic and their interests and curiosity
They carry out and register a monitored and directed research;
Apply practical investigation techniques such as Observation, Case Study and Interviews;
They develop an analysis, reflection and research report.

Section-4

Citizen Project Video preview 45 Min

Students map their identity, personality, interests and skills;
They explore their dreams and define their life project;
They conduct interviews to get to know the reality of higher education and professions up close;
They learn about important tools for the world of work (CV, personal pitch, LinkedIn, among others).

Section-5
